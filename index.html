Famous Dish

The omelet is one of the most loved and versatile dishes in the world.It is prepared by whisking fresh eggs until they become smooth and fluffy.The mixture is then seasoned with a pinch of salt and black pepper.Some people also add a dash of milk to make it extra soft and creamy.Once the batter is ready, it is poured onto a hot, greased pan.The sizzling sound and aroma instantly awaken the appetite.The surface starts to set while the bottom turns golden brown.At this stage, delicious fillings can be added to enhance the flavor.
